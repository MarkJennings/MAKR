RAS A-Frame


The UT Robotics and Automation Society (RAS) taught me to build my first robot, and I've been hooked ever since. In order to spread the word as much as possible, I built a wooden sign with the club's name, meeting dates, and website. One thing I learned from this is that painting takes forever. After the design was done, I protected it with several coats of sealant and put the sign outside. It survived Hurricane Harvey with no signs of wear, so I think it should last for years to come.
