BloomBot


BloomBot is an all-wheel drive robot intended to create large-scale land art. It has 6 tubes that can be filled with different types of seeds to plant differently colored flowers, grains of rice, or other types of plants. It is GPS navigated and capable of climbing 40 degree inclines under full load. This project was build with an artist who currently lives in Seattle, so I don't know its current state. The mechanical design is pretty robust, but it could definitely use some improvements.
