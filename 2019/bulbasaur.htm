Bulbasaur Planter


I printed a Bulbasaur figure that's pretty popular on 3d printing forums.

This wasn't an ambitious engineering project or anything, but it makes my smile whenever I see it.

I used "marble" filament and this design from Thingiverse. It took about 14 hours.

The succulent hasn't died yet!
